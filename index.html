Our Product And Our Values

We produce playing-card sized recipes which is split so that both children and adults can enjoy cooking the evening meal together! Our prime values encompass healthy eating and all recipes produced by us reflect this. We always work hard to ensure that all recipes are both child and adult friendly and comply with the recommended intake for a healthy diet.

YOUNG ENTERPRISE

We are part of the Young Enterprise scheme which is a not-for-profit business and enterprise education charity in England & Wales. It is made up of three organisations, Young Enterprise, Young Enterprise Scotland and Young Enterprise Northern Ireland, the latter two operating individually under a licence agreement to the former. Young Enterprise's mission statement is "to inspire and equip young people to learn and succeed through enterprise."
